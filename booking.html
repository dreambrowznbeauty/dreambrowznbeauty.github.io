Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Can I walk in without an appointment?

Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome. However, booking an appointment ensures you won't have to wait and guarantees your preferred time slot.

❓ How far in advance should I book?

You can book up to 3 months in advance. For last-minute appointments, check availability or call for walk-in availability.

❓ Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes! You can manage your appointment directly through the confirmation email. For changes within 24 hours, please call us for the smoothest experience.

❓ What should I prepare before my appointment?

Come with clean skin, avoid heavy makeup before facials, and skip caffeine before lash treatments if possible. Arrive 5 minutes early if it's your first visit.

❓ Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! For group appointments or special requests, please contact me directly at the contact information on the About page.

Can't Find Your Time Slot?

If you can't find the perfect time online, reach out directly and I'll do my best to accommodate you.
